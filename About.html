About Us

BAIA is an educational institution located in Bujumbura, Burundi. It has been in service since 2014. We opened with approximately 155 students. In April 2015 there was political unrest and the school closed down until September 2015 when it reopened with roughly 80 students. In the past three years the school has maintained an average enrollment of 145 students.
